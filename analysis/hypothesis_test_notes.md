# Hypothesis Test

## Null Hypothesis (H0)

The Treatment onboarding experience does not improve Paid Conversion Rate.

Control Conversion Rate = Treatment Conversion Rate

## Alternative Hypothesis (H1)

The Treatment onboarding experience improves Paid Conversion Rate.

Treatment Conversion Rate > Control Conversion Rate

## Significance Level

α = 0.05

## Test Used

Two-Proportion Z-Test

Reason:
Paid conversion is a binary outcome (0 = No, 1 = Yes).

## Test Results

Control Conversions = 22

Control Users = 693

Treatment Conversions = 50

Treatment Users = 715

Z-score = 3.252

P-value = 0.00057


## Decision

Since p-value (0.00057) is less than α (0.05), the null hypothesis is rejected.

## Business Interpretation

The Treatment onboarding experience generated a statistically significant improvement in Paid Conversion Rate.

The observed increase is unlikely to have occurred by chance and provides strong evidence that the Treatment performs better than the Control experience.
